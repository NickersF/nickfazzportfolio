STAR BAR PORTLAND CLIENT AND TECHNICAL OVERVIEW

Star Bar Portland is a rock'n'roll night life hotspot in inner Southeast Portland. Run with DIY ethics in mind I partnered with the owner to develop a simple, fast, and to-the-point website which serves the purpose of getting people to the bar for fun, and showcases the visual feel of the bar using an Instagram feed.

BLACK WATER BAR PORTLAND CLIENT AND TECHNICAL OVERVIEW

Black Water PDX is a vegan restaurant, bar, and music venue located in NE Portland. When beginning the development of the website the owners and I decided to build the website around the physical brand of the venue to establish a consistent feel. The website is designed mobile-first to deliver a fast, lightweight browsing experience on mobile devices.
